How To Apply

Things You Should Know

  • We do not accept people applying through Direct Messages (DMs).

  • You must submit an application to apply to a squadron. We (soon will) accept applicants applying through this website.

  • You will not be verified until your server display name or nickname is changed to your War Thunder in-game username.

  • It may take up to a week or more to be accepted. This could be the result of a large number of applications, the squadron reaching full capacity, or an issue with your application. At the time being, you can either wait for an open spot, or join one of our brother squadrons.

  • Read and accept the server's rules here to be accepted into a squadron.

  • Feel free to contact us here if you have any questions!
