How To Apply

Things You Should Know

  • We do not accept people applying though Direct Messages (DMs).

  • We only accept new applicants applying through the application forms on this website.

  • You must submit your application to apply to a squadron.

  • You will not be verified until your server display name or nickname will be changed to your War Thunder in-game username.

  • It may take up to a week to be accepted due to high volumes of applications, the squadron may be full, or your application was invalid.

  • Read and accept the server's rules here to be accepted into a squadron.

  • Feel free to contact us here if you have any questions!

Squadron Membership

  1. Visit the Squadron Membership page.

  2. Enter your Discord username. NOT your Discord display name or nickname

  3. Enter your War Thunder in-game username.

  4. Choose which squadron you wish to apply for.

  5. Click "Apply" to send your application. You should shortly recieve a notification on the same page if your application was submitted successfully or not. You can also check if you have a [SQUAD] Pending Verification role.
