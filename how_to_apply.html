How To Apply

Things You Should Know

  • Read and accept the server rules to be accepted into a squadron.

  • You will need to be verified after joining the Discord server to have squadron priority.

  • Your server display name or nickname needs to be changed to your War Thunder in-game username. (In the future, this can be done automatically by submitting an application.)

  • Submit an application to apply to a squadron. We (soon will) accept applicants applying through this website. We do not accept people applying through Direct Messages (DMs).

  • It may take up to a week or more to be accepted. This could be the result of the squadron reaching full member capacity, a high demand of applications, or an issue with your application. At the time being, you can either wait for an available spot or join one of our brother squadrons.

  • Feel free to contact us if you have any issues or questions!
