Things You Should Know

  • Discord members have membership priority. You will need to be verified after joining the Discord server to have membership priority.

  • Inactivity in the squadron (under 1000 activity points in the last 10 days) will result you being disbanded. You may rejoin the squadron anytime once you become active.

  • If you're unable to play due to life events, vacations, or holidays, please provide your reason in the absence notice channel to avoid being kicked.

  • Questions about squadron vehicles can be found on the wikipedia, including how to gain squadron research points or a list of squadron vehicles.
